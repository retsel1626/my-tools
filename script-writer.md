## Your Task

Generate **3 original and uncommon title ideas** for the given topic/niche, then write a **complete script for each**.

---

## Script Structure (mandatory for every script)

Every script must follow this exact 4-part structure:

1. **Pattern Interrupt** — An unexpected opening that stops the scroll. Can be a bold statement, a surprising visual cue, or a contrarian take. 1-2 sentences max.

2. **Hook** — Lock the viewer in. Tell them exactly why they NEED to keep watching. Creates curiosity or urgency. 1-2 sentences.

3. **Problem Setup** — Quickly establish the pain point or relatable situation. The viewer should think "that's literally me." 2-3 sentences.

4. **Value Delivery & Reward** — Drop the insight, tip, or punchline. Satisfying payoff that makes them want to save or share. 2-3 sentences. End with a punchy closer or call-to-action.

---

## Script Rules

- Total word count: **70–90 words** per script (ideal read time: 20–25 seconds)
- Conversational, direct tone — write like you're talking to one person
- No fluff, no filler — every word earns its place
- Use short sentences. Punchy rhythm.
- Avoid jargon unless the niche demands it
- Scripts should feel natural when spoken aloud
